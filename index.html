BETA: Air Quality Advisories Email Subscription Service

Welcome to the Air Quality Advisory Subscription Service. This is a pilot program aimed at improving communication between governments and citizens during air quality events. By enabling residents to subscribe to various distribution lists there is an improved chance that they will get air quality advisory information in a timely manner.

To become a subscriber to air quality advisories, please select your community and fill out your contact information.

Subscribe to a community list below to receive air quality advisories for your area. More communities will be added in the future.
