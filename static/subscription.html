BETA: Air Quality Advisories Email Subscription Service

Welcome to the Air Quality Advisory Subscription Service. This goal of the program is to improve communication between governments and citizens during air quality events in a timely manner. We currently have two notification services: Air Quality Advisories and Smoky Skies Bulletin.

To become a subscriber to air quality advisories, please select your community and fill out your contact information.

1. Enter and confirm your email address

How do I subscribe?

To begin, enter your email address followed by selecting the communities and regions you would like to subscribe to for Air Quality Adivsories and Smoky Skies Bulletins. Click on the check boxes to make your selection.

2. Choose an Air Quality Advisory

Air quality advisories are issued for the following communities below. More communities will be added in the future. Learn more.

If you do not see your specific community, you could select the closest one.

3. Choose a Smoky Skies Bulletin

These bulletins are a special type of public advisory to communicate the rapidly changing nature of wildfire smoke. Learn more.

For your convienience, the Smoky Skies Bulletin region list below matches up with the Air Quality Advisory community subscription list on the left.

Privacy and Collection Notice
