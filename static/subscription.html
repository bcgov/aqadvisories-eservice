Air Quality Subscription Service

Subscribe to receive notifications of Air Quality Advisories and Smoky Skies Bulletins for your area.

1. How do you want to be notified?

Choose either method or both.

(to receive text message notifications)

Note: Standard rates charged by your carrier may apply.

How do I subscribe?

  • Step 1. Subscribe to receive either email notifications, SMS text message notifications or both.
  • Step 2. Select the communities and regions you would like to receive notifications for
  • Step 3. Review the information you are submitting and then select the Subscribe button
  • Step 4. You will receive an email and/or text message asking you to confirm your subscription. Follow the instructions in the message to complete your subscription.

Learn more about the Air Quality Subscription Service.

2. Subscribe to Air Quality Advisories and Smoky Skies Bulletins

Air quality advisories and Smoky Skies Bulletins are issued for the following communities and regions below. Learn the diferences between Air quality advisories and Smoky Skies Bulletins on our Air Advisories page.

More communities with Air Quality Advisories may be added in the future. If you do not see your specific community, you can select the closest one. Other communities/regions that only have Smoky Skies bulletins are listed on the right.

Are you unsure which Smoky Skies region you fall under? Click on a region name to open our weather forecast regional map or your can access the map here. Return to this page after you have found your region's name.
