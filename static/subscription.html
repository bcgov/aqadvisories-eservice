Air Quality Subscription Service

Subscribe to receive notifications of Air Quality Advisories and Smoky Skies Bulletins for your area. Learn more about these types of notifications.

1. Enter and confirm your email address

How do I subscribe?

  • Step 1. Enter and confirm your email address
  • Step 2. Select the communities and regions you would like to receive notifications for
  • Step 3. Select the Subscribe button
  • Step 4. You will receive an email (asking you to confirm your subscription). Follow the instructions in the email to complete your subscription.

* The option to subscribe and receive SMS (text messages) notifications to your phone is currently in development.

Learn more about the Air Quality Subscription Service

2. Subscribe to Air Quality Advisories and/or Smoky Skies Bulletins

Air quality advisories and Smoky Skies Bulletins are issued for the following communities below. More communities with Air Quality Advisories may be added in the future. If you do not see your specific community, you could select the closest one. Other communities that only have Smoky Skies bulletins are listed on the right.

Are you unsure which Smoky Skies region you fall under? Click on a community name to open our weather forecast regional map or your can access the map here. Return to this page after you have found your region's name.
