Air Quality Health Index Alerts - Air Quality Subscription Service

Subscribe to receive notifications of Elevated Air Quality Health Index Alerts for your area.

1. Enter and confirm your contact information

How do I subscribe?

  • Step 1. Enter and confirm your email address.
  • Step 2. Select the communities and regions you would like to receive notifications for
  • Step 3. Review the information you are submitting and then select the Subscribe button
  • Step 4. You will receive an email asking you to confirm your subscription. Follow the instructions in the message to complete your subscription.
